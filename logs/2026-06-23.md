## Motion Design Direction Update

Changed SODA's page-level motion from bouncy spring to smooth ease-in-out bezier. Goal: relaxed, enjoyable, clickable experience — user notices the smoothness, not the motion.

**Modal/drawer curve:** `cubic-bezier(0.45, 0.05, 0.55, 0.95)` at 260–340ms
