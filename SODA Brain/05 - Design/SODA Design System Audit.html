What holds up: the nudge privacy boundary is honored everywhere, color semantics are mostly disciplined, the brand-agnostic rule reads correctly, the voice survives the build, and all 10 new screens are faithful to their prompts.

The four P1 spec breaks:

  1. Identity fields never lock — the Event Layer's draft/live/closed editing rule isn't enforced in the Admin
  2. Command Center can fire acts in any state — no lifecycle gating, the misfire protection the docs call out
  3. Lifecycle message has only one of its two variations — "event-ended" (with the survey path) is missing
  4. Sign-in collects phone + Google identity that the privacy policy's "that is the full list" doesn't cover — a decision needed in one direction or the other

Biggest debt: the states pass — 4 of the 5 "happens at every event" states are unbuilt, including the early room ("the most important empty state in the app"). Plus six components the inventory marks "Built" that don't exist in this system's kits.
