The follow-up loop
● What warmth decay drives, over time
78

Warmth decays

Every contact cools on the platform curve as days pass without contact. Saved and matched people carry intent, casual ones cool quietly.
↓
●

A follow-up nudge surfaces

trigger: time passes and an intent contact cools past the threshold. A purple nudge appears in the Contacts overview and on the tab.
↓
↻

Reach out, or dismiss

Reaching out refreshes warmth to full and clears the nudge. Dismissing is logged as a signal, so the system learns who not to nudge. SODA never messages on the guest’s behalf.
