**What & when:** A small mono pill carrying a documentation status, a contact follow-up signal, or a freeform tone. Meaning is word + color, not an icon.

```jsx
<Badge status="built">Built</Badge>
<Badge status="spec">Specified</Badge>
<Badge signal="reached">Reached out</Badge>
<Badge signal="nudge">Reach out</Badge>
<Badge tone="green" dot>3 here</Badge>
```
