What Simple Mode is for

It shrinks the first build to the two scan moments, scan to get in and scan to leave feedback, with a quick profile in between. No live timing, no real-time pairing to hold for hours, no critical mass needed in the room.

What you gain

Far less to build and far less to break. You prove the entry and feedback loop at a real event before betting on the acts. The home, contacts, and follow-up nudge still work from whoever scanned in.

What goes quiet

The Drop, the Chance, and the Nudge, the moments that make SODA feel different from a sign-in sheet. Simple Mode proves the plumbing; the acts are what prove the product.

The middle ground: the acts are individual toggles too, so a host can turn the Room View back on, or just the Drop, without going all the way to Full. It is a dial, not a switch.

A name tag knows you showed up. SODA knows who you became to the room, even on the simplest night.
