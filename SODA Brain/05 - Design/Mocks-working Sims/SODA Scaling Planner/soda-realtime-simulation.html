One phone, one jack. Or one phone, two.

Every guest's phone needs to hear two things during your event: The Drop (the room-wide prompt) and The Nudge (their private match signal). The only question that matters: does each phone use one jack to hear both, or grab two jacks? Supabase counts jacks (connections), not what you listen to over them.
