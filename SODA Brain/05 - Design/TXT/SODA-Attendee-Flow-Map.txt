SODA . ATTENDEE FLOW MAP (TEXT VERSION)
The Attendee Journey, Across the Event Lifecycle
A product of Equalpoint, Inc. . Current as of the Event Layer update

Every screen a guest can reach, and what moves them forward. Refreshed to
fold in the event lifecycle, so it shows the live path plus what a guest
sees when they scan before doors open or after the event closes.

LEGEND
  [N]   main step in the live journey
  (B)   branch off the main path
  {L}   lifecycle state or lifecycle-driven screen


================================================================
THE EVENT LIFECYCLE
================================================================
The lifecycle state decides which path a scan leads to. The host flips
it from the Admin.

    {DRAFT}  ......>  {LIVE}  ......>  {CLOSED}
   host setting up   doors open,      event ended,
   QR not yet live   journey runs     survey + send-off remain


================================================================
WHEN THE SCAN HAPPENS, THE LIFECYCLE DECIDES
================================================================

{L} THE SCAN
    A guest scans the event QR. What they see next depends on the event state:
      - draft  -> not-yet-open message
      - live   -> the journey (below)
      - closed -> event-ended message

      (B) BRANCH . event not live
          {L} LIFECYCLE MESSAGE
              Before doors open, a warm not-yet-open note in the host identity.
              After close, an event-ended note with a path to the survey.
              This is the new screen the lifecycle adds.


================================================================
THE LIVE JOURNEY
================================================================

[1] ENTRY QR
     trigger: guest scans while the event is live.
     note: the eyebrow shows the event name pulled from the event record.
      |
      v
[2] WELCOME
     trigger: tap to begin.
     note: wears the host logo, or the host name in type if no logo.
      |
      |   (B) BRANCH . returning guest
      |       (R) RECOGNITION
      |           A known guest is greeted by name with their history, then
      |           enters the room directly, skipping setup.
      |           Rejoins the main path at the Room View [8].
      v
[3] SIGN-IN
     trigger: system verifies the six-digit code sent to email.
      |
      v
[4] PHOTO
     trigger: add photo or skip. either advances.
      |
      |   (B) BRANCH . skip the photo
      |       (S) INITIALS AVATAR
      |           Skipping generates a colored initials avatar and moves on
      |           with no friction. A first-class choice.
      v
[5] ROLE CHIPS
     trigger: pick a role, then continue.   (step 1 of 3)
      |
      v
[6] OFFER CHIPS
     trigger: pick what you offer, then continue.   (step 2 of 3)
      |
      v
[7] NEED CHIPS
     trigger: pick what you need, then enter the room.   (step 3 of 3)
      |
      v
[8] ROOM VIEW
     The home base. The three acts are launched into this screen by the host.
     Re-entry by email returns here.
      |
      v
[9] THE DROP   (Act 1)
     trigger: host fires the prompt to every phone at once.
     Answer, then watch the wall fill.
      |
      v
[10] THE CHANCE   (Act 2)
     trigger: host pairs the room.
     A countdown runs in amber. Go talk to your match.
      |
      v
[11] THE NUDGE   (Act 3)
     trigger: a private match arrives for one person only.
     One person, one reason.


================================================================
AFTER THE EVENT CLOSES
================================================================

[12] SURVEY
     trigger: reached by survey QR or recap email. available in the closed state.
      |
      v
[13] SEND-OFF
     trigger: survey complete.
     Warm close in the host identity, soft prompt to the next step.
      |
      v
[14] RECAP EMAIL
     trigger: sent automatically at event end.
     Profile card, matches, recap link. Wears the host identity.


================================================================
THE THREE BRANCHES, IN SHORT
================================================================
  - Returning guest: at Welcome [2], a known guest sees Recognition (R)
    first, then enters the room directly because their profile exists.
  - Skip the photo: at Photo [4], skipping (S) makes an initials avatar
    and moves on with no friction.
  - Re-entry by email: a guest who reopens the link mid-event returns
    straight to the Room View [8] without repeating sign-in.

A name tag knows you showed up. SODA knows who you became to the room.
