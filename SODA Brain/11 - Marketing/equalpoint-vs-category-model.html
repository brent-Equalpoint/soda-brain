The strategic line

A pipe ends. A loop compounds.

Blinq automates the writing. Equal Point automates the remembering. Their AI got good at drafting the follow-up — but the trigger to act still sits on the user, so the lead quietly cools while the AI waits to be asked. Equal Point starts where their model ends: warmth decay surfaces who's going cold, the system pings you, then drafts. They answer "what do I say?" We answer "who do I owe, and when?" They sell the introduction. We hold the whole relationship.
