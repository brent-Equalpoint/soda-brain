The state-aware hero is the whole point. The server sets hero.state to live or idle and fills the matching copy. The client renders whichever state it receives, it never decides on its own whether a room is live. That decision is the server's, computed the same way resolveAttendeeScreen computes which screen an attendee sees. Swap one field, the home screen transforms.
